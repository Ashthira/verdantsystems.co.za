Information we do not collect:

  • Financial or payment information

How we protect your information

We take reasonable and appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • HTTPS encryption on our website — all data transmitted to and from this website is encrypted in transit
  • Security headers including Content Security Policy, HSTS, and X-Frame-Options to protect against common web attacks
  • Access to your information is limited to the owner of Verdant Systems only
  • Use of reputable third-party processors (Formspree and Zoho) who maintain their own security standards

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we take your privacy seriously and implement reasonable safeguards, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
